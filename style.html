DSA Overview

DSA (Data Structures and Algorithms) is a foundational concept in computer science and programming. It focuses on organizing, managing, and storing data efficiently and performing operations on that data in an optimal way. Mastering DSA is crucial for solving complex computational problems and plays a significant role in technical interviews for software development roles.
